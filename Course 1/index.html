Phasmophobia Evidences to Identify Ghosts

Are you eager to learn the secrets of identifying the many ghosts in the game?

With this guide, we will show you how to do just that!

Tools

Let's get used to the large variety of tools available in the game.

Depending on the difficulty, each ghost have from 3-4 evidences to only 1-2 evidences (the additional evidence is the lying "Mimic"-ghost).

The EMF

The EMF is perhaps the most notorious tool in the game, used to measure the levels of radioactivity. It is a great tool to make sure you are in the ghost room and to identify the ghost.

Ghosts that have EMF level 5 as an evidence:
Spirit
Wraith
Jinn
Shade
Oni
Goryo
Myling
The Twins
Raiju
Obake

An EMF.

The Ultraviolet Flashlight

The ultraviolet flashlight is used to spot fingerprints. It is excellent for spotting ectoplasmic fingerprints or footprints left by the ghost. Obake, one of the notorious ghosts, has a special interaction with the ultraviolet flashlight, sometimes showing a unique handprint with six fingers or a seriously twisted foot. Ghosts that have ultraviolet:
Phantom
Poltergeist
Banshee
Jinn
Demon
Hantu
Goryo
Myling
Obake
The Mimic

Ghost fingerprints revealed under UV light.

D.O.T.S

The D.O.T.S Projectoris a device that can show movement in ghosts, allowing the players to eventually see a ghost model if the projector is aimed at the area where the ghost is. The Goryo cannot be seen through in-person projection, so one must use a camera (which is highlighted in the coming section. The ghosts that have D.O.T.S as an evidence are:

Ghost Orbs

The Ghost Orbs can be seen with a video camera . The orbs are orbs that have a snowfall like animation and are often the giveaway of a ghost room if the ghost has ghost orb as an evidence. The Mimic is a liar, and will always have ghost orb as an evidence even when he does not explicitly have it as an evidence. The ghosts that have Ghost Orbs are as following:

Mare

The Mimic LIAR
Thaye

Spirit Box

The Spirit Box is a type of evidence that interacts with the microphone, allowing you to talk to the ghost with common phrases like "Are you friendly?", "How many have you killed?", "Where are you?" and other phrases to converse with the ghost. If the ghost speaks back, it's a spirit-boxing ghost. The following ghosts have Spirit Box as an evidence:
Spirit
Wraith
Phantom
Poltergeist
Mare
Yokai
Onryo
